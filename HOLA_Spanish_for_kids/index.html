About us

“HOLA” is a program that allows preschoolers in Cleveland to learn Spanish naturally and it’s easy as uno, dos tres! We use state of the art methods.
Our mission is to help your child to learn Spanish in a natural, fun and interactive way through immersion and play. We believe that students learn best when they are relaxed and having fun.
Our teachers are native bilingual Spanish and English speakers with educational experience in preschool child development programs.

Benefits

Learning Spanish in America today should empower your children as we all become more connected with Latin America, its people, culture and business interactions.
